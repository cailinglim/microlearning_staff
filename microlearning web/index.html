Phishing & Email Safety

Phishing attacks are one of the most common cybersecurity threats in healthcare settings. Attackers send deceptive emails that appear to come from trusted sources — tricking you into revealing credentials or clicking malicious links.

Key Stat: Over 90% of data breaches in healthcare start with a phishing email. One click can compromise an entire network.

Phishing emails often create a sense of urgency — "Your account will be suspended in 24 hours." This pressure is intentional, designed to make you act before you think.

Red Flags in a Phishing Email

Sender mismatch · Urgent language · Suspicious links · Generic greeting · Odd attachments
Never enter your password after clicking a link in an email. Always navigate directly to the site by typing the URL yourself.
